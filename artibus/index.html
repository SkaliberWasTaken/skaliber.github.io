Consistent Leather

Animals that drop leather will consistently drop a single leather item regardless of looting.

More Shulker Shells

End Cities are far apart, and the end is quite dull. Shulkers always drop at least one of their shells, and regularly drop both.

Silk Touch Vines

Vines can be collected with either shears or silk touch.

Zombie Nuggets

Zombies no longer drop iron ingots, but drop iron nuggets at a considerably higher rate.
